Our Story...

Basketballs_My_Player has allowed
avid and authenticated basketball fans
the ability to create, read, update, and delete
both shoes and players! Users are also able
to add games for each player created
(a one to many relationship between
players and games). AWS is included as well to allow users the ability to insert pictures for their players!
