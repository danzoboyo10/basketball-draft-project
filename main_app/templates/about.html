Our Story...

Basketballs_My_Player has allowed avid
and authenticated basketball fans the
ability to create, read, update, and delete
both shoes and players! In addition, users are
able to add games for each player created
(a one to many relationship between players
and games). AWS is included as well to allow users
the ability to insert pictures for the location of their games!
